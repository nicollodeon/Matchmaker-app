FAQs

How does Finder work?
Think of Finder as a speed dating application. Our main goal is to match you up with your next significant other. We do this by queueing you into a pool of online users, of which you'll be matched with someone - but there's a twist! Whilst you'll be able to chat with that user, you won't be able to see their profile and only their username. Should you choose to match with them, you'll be able to see their profile page and all their information!
What sets it apart from other dating sites?
Our site attempts to remove as many biases as we can from the dating experience. Users will not get to see the other user until after they match! This is so that users will focus on the personality aspect of dating rather than the physical.
Can I meet new friend there?
This website is meant for dating! Please don't come here to find new friends.
Do I need to pay for the service?
All our services are free! Enjoy your stay.
